Read & share results

Results in plain language — shared on your terms.

Every lab opens with reference ranges, clear flags, and a friendly note about what to watch. When you want a doctor to see it, you generate a secure, time-boxed link that shows only what you pick and expires on schedule. You can revoke access at any moment.

  • Human-readable lab detailValues, optimal ranges, and a note on anything borderline — no jargon.
  • Secure, expiring sharesChoose the doctor, the documents, and an expiry of 24 hours, 7 days or 30 days.
  • Always revocableAn access log shows exactly who viewed what, and one tap pulls it all back.

Privacy & security

Your health is yours. Full stop.

EaseMyDisease is built privacy-first: your vault is encrypted on your device, locked behind biometrics, and never sold. You see who accessed what, and you can export or revoke everything at any time.

End-to-end encrypted

Your vault is encrypted on your device. Not even we can read it without your key.

Biometric lock

Face ID or fingerprint is required to open your vault, every time.

Access log

See exactly who viewed your records and when — and revoke all shared access in one tap.

Export anytime

Download a full copy of your data in standard FHIR or PDF formats. It's portable, because it's yours.

HIPAA-compliant

Handling and storage meet HIPAA standards, with consent baked into every share.

Three languages

English, Spanish and Hindi throughout — including live translation during consults.
